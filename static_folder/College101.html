Need to Knows about going to College

Picking a Major

Have no idea what you want to study? That's Okay! You do not have to know right away; some schools even make you wait until your second year to declare a major. Best thing to do in this situation is to find what what's you happy. That means taking classes on subjects you find interesting and joining different clubs. And You can always, always, always change your major if it's not what you want.

How do I get Financial Aid?

First, dont panic. It many be overwhelming, but take it slow: it will be okay.
You can get Financial aid through the Federal government (FAFSA), State governments, your college, and private organizations. In order to access this money you have to APPLY!

Start with appyling for FAFSA. By doing so you can get acces to Grants/Scholarships, Work-Study jobs, and government loans that you will usually be able to pay back after graduation. Next apply for Financial Aid at the college you will be attending. Then start looking for and APPLYING for scholarships.

Scholarships

Why should I apply for Scholarships?

  1. Lets be honest. College costs a lot of money and tuition keeps increasing.

  4. They are free to apply to and can cover tuition costs that financial aid from the government and your school many not.
