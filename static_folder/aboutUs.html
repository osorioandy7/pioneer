The people behind The Pioneers

Andy Osorio

Hello, Andy here. I worked on the back end of the application alongside Rebecca. I, as a first generation college student, feel that knowledge is key to understanding what it means to pursue higher education. I know I had no idea what I was doing when I was introduced into the idea of college. My mom had little to no education so I had to rely on the staff of my high school to help me out with the entire concept of college. I am passionate about this project because I believe it can provide an opportunity to other students who are in the same position I was once in. I hope that this project paves a way for aspiring first generation college students in the future.

Denise Chacanaca

Hola, yo soy Denise! This website means the world to me because both my parents never went to college or attempted it, however, I am so fortunate to have my older brother to guide me through the college process. I know not everyone has that same resource so through this incredible process we've worked side by side to show you the way through the applying process of college. Let us be your pioneers.

Rebecca Zeng

Hi! My name is Rebecca, and I worked on the forum backend and Google App Engine elements of this project. I am a first generation college student; my parents arrived in the U.S. with virtually neither money nor English skills, so I owe everything I do to their diligence and dedication to my future when I was growing up. Aside from studying computer science languages, I also passionately desire exposure to world languages in an attempt to learn more about different cultures and human interactions. Ultimately, this project enabled me to learn from others and utilize my own knowledge in order to benefit other first generation college students.
